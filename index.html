My Favorite Media Outlets

Sixth Tone

Sixth Tone is a Chinese publication which gives fresh takes on trending topics in modern China. I like to read this English-language site to get a better understanding of the complexities and nuances in society, culture, technology, education, and business in the Middle Kingdom.

The New York Times

Though I'm not a huge fan of their increasingly biased and sloppy journalism, I use the NYTimes to better understand the perspective of liberal and moderate America.

Politico

When I need to get a more balanced and insider perspective of what is going on in American politics, I turn to Politico.
